# 04. Document Analysis

## Introduction

During a criminal investigation, investigating officers are required to prepare multiple legal and procedural documents at different stages of the case. These documents ensure proper documentation of evidence, legal compliance, and communication with judicial and medical authorities.

The objective of this document is to study the purpose, structure, and workflow of these documents so they can be generated automatically using AI while maintaining consistency with official formats.

The references collected for this study include official government forms, police manuals, investigation guides, medico-legal manuals, and sample investigation documents.

---

# 1. First Information Report (FIR)

## Purpose

The First Information Report (FIR) is the first official document registered after receiving information about a cognizable offence. It formally initiates the criminal investigation and records the details of the complaint.

## Prepared By

Investigating Officer / Duty Officer

## Generated During

Immediately after complaint registration.

## Information Required

- Complaint Details
- Complainant Information
- Incident Details
- Victim Information
- Suspect Information (if available)
- Place and Time of Incident
- Applicable Legal Sections
- Police Station Details

## Generated by CaseCraftAI

✅ Yes

The system generates a structured FIR draft after analyzing the complaint and retrieving relevant legal sections through the RAG pipeline.

The investigating officer reviews, edits, and approves the final draft before registration.

- Official Blank FIR Template

- Sample FIR (Cheating)

# 2. Remand Request

## Purpose

A Remand Request is submitted before the court to seek police or judicial custody of the accused for further investigation. It explains why additional custody is necessary and describes the pending investigative activities.

## Prepared By

Investigating Officer

## Generated During

After arrest, when further interrogation or investigation requires custody.

## Information Required

- FIR Number
- Court Details
- Police Station
- Accused Details
- Investigation Summary
- Grounds for Remand
- Duration Requested
- Officer Details

## Generated by CaseCraftAI

✅ Yes

CaseCraftAI prepares a structured draft using information already available in the Case Data Pool. Officers can modify investigation-specific reasoning before submission.

## Reference Documents

- Sample Remand Application
- Remand Application Template

# 3. Medical Examination Request

## Purpose

A Medical Examination Request is issued to request examination of the victim or accused. The examination helps document injuries, collect forensic evidence, and provide expert medical opinion required during investigation.

## Prepared By

Investigating Officer

## Generated During

Whenever medical examination becomes necessary during investigation.

## Information Required

- FIR Number
- Police Station
- Victim / Accused Details
- Hospital Details
- Examination Purpose
- Investigation Reference
- Officer Details

## Generated by CaseCraftAI

✅ Yes

The platform generates a medical examination request by automatically filling available case information into a predefined template.

## Reference Documents

- Medical Examination of Injured Person Form

# 4. Property Seizure Memo

## Purpose

A Property Seizure Memo records the seizure of articles, documents, or other evidence collected during investigation. It provides a legally documented record of seized property and the circumstances under which it was recovered.

## Prepared By

Investigating Officer

## Generated During

Whenever evidence or property is seized.

## Information Required

- FIR Number
- Property Details
- Place of Seizure
- Witness Details
- Person from whom Property was Seized
- Date and Time
- Investigating Officer

## Generated by CaseCraftAI

✅ Yes

# 5. Panchanama

## Purpose

A Panchanama is an official record documenting observations made during search, seizure, recovery, or inspection in the presence of independent witnesses (Panchas). It serves as an important evidentiary document during investigation and court proceedings.

## Prepared By

Investigating Officer

## Generated During

During search, seizure, recovery, or other investigative proceedings requiring independent witnesses.

## Information Required

- FIR Number
- Date and Time
- Location
- Witness Details
- Investigation Observations
- Articles Recovered
- Officer Details

## Generated by CaseCraftAI

⚠️ Partially

The platform prepares the initial document using available case information. Since observations are highly situation-specific, officers complete and verify the narrative before finalization.

## Reference Documents

- Panchanama Guidelines
- Discovery Panchanama (Section 27)

# Conclusion

The analysis of these investigation documents demonstrates that a significant portion of police documentation follows standardized formats and repeatedly uses the same case information throughout the investigation lifecycle. This creates an opportunity to automate repetitive documentation while maintaining legal accuracy and consistency.

CaseCraftAI leverages a centralized **Case Data Pool** to eliminate redundant data entry and assist investigating officers in generating structured drafts of commonly used documents such as the FIR, Remand Request, Medical Examination Request, Property Seizure Memo, and Panchanama.

Rather than replacing human decision-making, the platform follows a **Human-in-the-Loop** approach, where AI assists by preparing drafts or auto-filling templates, while investigating officers remain responsible for reviewing, editing, and approving every document before it becomes part of the official case record.

The collected reference documents and manuals provide valuable guidance for designing templates that closely resemble official police documentation while supporting faster, more consistent, and efficient investigation workflows.
